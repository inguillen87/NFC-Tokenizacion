PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
Build a premium B2C consumer portal and cross-tenant premium product ecosystem for nexID.

nexID must become:
- A traceability and authenticity SaaS for brands.
- A consumer Digital Product Passport wallet.
- A loyalty and rewards portal.
- A verified premium product marketplace.
- A cross-brand discovery ecosystem for authenticated physical products.
- A consumer relationship layer for each tenant.
- A LATAM/global premium network for wineries, events, perfumes, cosmetics, luxury goods and retailers.

STRATEGIC PRODUCT THESIS
When a consumer taps a verified NFC product from a tenant, they should be able to:
1. Verify authenticity.
2. Register or log in as a nexID consumer.
3. Join that tenant’s loyalty program.
4. Associate the product/tap with their personal portal.
5. Earn tenant-specific points or rewards if eligible.
6. See product/tap history.
7. Redeem tenant rewards such as wine bottles, wine boxes, discounts, tasting upgrades, promos or VIP experiences.
8. Discover other verified premium brands inside the nexID ecosystem.
9. Opt in to connect with other tenants and receive offers.
10. Build a personal collection of authentic premium products.

This must work especially well for:
- Mendoza wineries
- Argentine wineries
- LATAM wineries
- Premium wine shops / vinotecas
- Premium events
- Perfumes
- Cosmetics / creams
- Luxury retail
- Export brands
- Resellers selling NFC chips and SaaS access to clients

DO NOT BUILD
- A fake marketplace.
- Fake purchases.
- Fake rewards.
- A single global points currency without liability controls.
- Cross-tenant data sharing without explicit opt-in.
- Gambling mechanics.
- Lottery mechanics.
- Alcohol rewards without age gate support.
- Any reward claim flow that can be triggered by replay/revoked/tampered/broken taps.

NON-NEGOTIABLE ARCHITECTURE RULES
1. Separate identity from tenant membership.
   A user can have one global nexID consumer account, but membership must be tenant-scoped.

2. Separate point ledgers.
   Use at least two ledger concepts:
   - Tenant points: owned/funded by one tenant.
   - NexID network credits: optional platform-level credits, disabled by default unless explicitly configured.

3. Cross-tenant marketplace is opt-in.
   A tenant must choose whether its offers/rewards/products appear in the nexID network marketplace.

4. Consumer data is private by default.
   Tenant A cannot see consumer activity with Tenant B unless the consumer explicitly opted into cross-brand sharing or marketplace participation.

5. Tap authenticity gates rewards.
   Only valid, non-replay, non-revoked, non-broken, non-tampered events can trigger points/rewards.

6. Marketplace orders are not payments unless payment provider exists.
   If there is no payment provider in the repo/env, implement:
   - request-to-buy
   - external checkout URL
   - lead/order request
   - reward redemption
   Do not invent payment processing.

7. Alcohol vertical requires age gate.
   For wine/alcohol tenants:
   - consumer must confirm legal age before alcohol reward redemption.
   - region/country eligibility must be configurable.
   - no gambling/chance mechanics.

8. Every reward has inventory/state.
   Rewards must support available, locked, claimed, redeemed, expired, cancelled and out_of_stock.

9. Every claim is auditable.
   Store consumer, tenant, tapEventId, rewardId, points ledger entry, redemption code, status changes, timestamps and source IP/user agent hashes if existing privacy model supports it.

10. The portal must feel premium.
   This is not a generic account page. It must feel like a luxury passport wallet for verified products.

PHASE 4 — POINTS AND REDEMPTION LOGIC
Implement these rules.

1. Tenant Points
- Points earned from valid taps belong to one tenant program.
- Tenant controls points name, earning rules, expiration, reward catalog, tier rules, transfer/pooling rules.
- Points cannot be spent in another tenant unless cross-tenant settlement is enabled.

2. NexID Network Credits
- Disabled by default.
- Optional platform-level incentive for profile completion, cross-brand onboarding, marketplace discovery, referrals and campaigns funded by nexID.
- Must have separate ledger.
- Do not mix with tenant liabilities.

3. Cross-Tenant Redemption
Allowed only if:
- tenant reward is marked networkVisible
- tenant accepts network credits or partner points
- settlementPolicy exists
- stock available
- consumer eligible
- consumer has consented to marketplace offers
- age/region rules pass

4. Wine Box / Bottle / Discount Redemption
